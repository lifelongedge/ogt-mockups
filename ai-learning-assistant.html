What is a Frog setup and when should I use it?

The Frog is one of Dr. Long's core volatility-based trading setups. Here's how it works:

What it measures

The Frog Trading Value represents the average daily range of a stock, expressed in dollars. It tells you "how far this stock typically jumps in a day."

When to use it

  • Use the Frog when you want to day trade or swing trade a volatile stock
  • The Frog value helps you set realistic profit targets and stop losses
  • A stock with a Frog of $8.40 (like SPY currently) means you can reasonably expect an $8.40 range on a normal day

Position sizing with the Frog

Dr. Long: "If the Frog is $8, and your risk per trade is $200, then your position size is 25 shares. The Frog tells you when to jump, not how far."

Current example

SPY's Frog(30d) is $8.40 (1.55% of price). This is in the "normal volatility" range. When it exceeds 2%, conditions favor wider stops and smaller positions.
