FAQ

What exactly is a “simulator”?
A small interactive web app for one trading concept. It has live controls (sliders, toggles), a chart that re-draws instantly off the real Edge math, a one-line coach caption that interprets what you're seeing, and a short completion check. You learn by doing, not by watching.
Do I need the Edge platform or a trading account?
No. The simulators run in your browser on synthetic data, so you can learn the concept with zero risk and no account. The Novice rung is built for people who haven't placed their first trade — and arguably matters most before you do.
How are the 149 simulators organized?
Fourteen domains, three levels (Novice → Intermediate → Skilled). Each sim is numbered so you always know where you are — and so feedback and discussion can reference a sim precisely. The full numbered catalog is one click away.
What's the Ask AI feature?
Every simulator has an Ask AI panel. Ask a question about the concept on screen and get an answer grounded in that specific lab's context — the title, the step you're on, and the numbers in front of you. It's a tutor that knows exactly what you're looking at.
Who is Dr. Ken Long?
Forty-year systematic trader, founder of Tortoise Capital, long-time coach to independent traders through the Owl Group cohort. Author of multiple published frameworks — Bandy risk-normalized sizing, CAR25 return modeling, the craftsman framework. Many of these simulators encode patterns he teaches live.
What's the refund policy?
30 days on single simulators, 60 days on the full lab — full refund, no questions. We want you to commit to the climb, not feel locked in.
