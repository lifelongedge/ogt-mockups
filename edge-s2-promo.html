3PROBLEM
Why another platform

You've outgrown your tools.

A charting site, a spreadsheet, a broker UI, and three browser tabs is not a trading operation. And the polished SaaS alternatives all share the same flaw — they end where the vendor's roadmap ends.

You rent your edge

Cancel the subscription and your studies, your scans, your history all vanish. You never owned any of it.

It's a black box

You can't see how the indicator is computed, whether the backtest leaks the future, or why two screens disagree. You just have to trust it.

You can't change it

Your edge is specific. The tool is generic. When you need one custom scan, one new indicator, one workflow tweak — you file a feature request and wait forever.

Your data isn't yours

Your fills, your watchlists, your journal — sitting on someone else's server, mined, and one breach away from public.

10FAQ
FAQ

Straight answers.

Is my data actually private?

On Self-Hosted, yes — completely. The platform runs on your hardware and your fills, watchlists, journals, and studies never leave your machine. On Cloud, your instance is single-tenant and isolated; we host it, but it's yours alone.

Will it trade for me?

It will paper-trade a portfolio on autopilot — so you can watch a system run hands-free and judge it before risking a cent — but it will never place a live order. Edge S2 is a second brain, not a bot; the trigger is always yours. If you want live automation, the platform is open and you can build it on top. We don't ship one out of the box.

Do I need to be a programmer?

No, to trade with it. The workbench, charts, scans, and systems are point-and-click. You only touch code when you want to customize — and even then, the built-in AI does most of the work against the platform's own guardrails while you review.

Where does the market data come from?

You bring your own data feed and AI key. The platform is built to ingest a real-time stream and end-of-day history, validate it daily, and keep it adjustment-correct. Your subscriptions stay yours.

Can it really not look ahead?

No-look-ahead is structural, not a setting you can forget. Research windows stop strictly before the decision bar, universes resolve point-in-time (delisted names included), and core math is pinned to blessed values. A result you get is one you can reproduce and defend.

What can I customize?

Indicators, views, systems, playbooks, scans, and alert rules — all first-class. Add an indicator once and it flows into every chart, scan, and API response automatically. With Self-Hosted you hold the full source.

What if I get stuck?

Every component is documented by an explicit contract, the platform ships with guides and a built-in AI assistant grounded in your setup, and Self-Hosted and Desk tiers include direct support.
