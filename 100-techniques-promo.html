Anatomy of a technique

Each technique is a loop. Not just a video.

The video is the start. The worksheet is where you write the technique into your own trading. The checklist locks it as a habit. The completion gate stops you from advancing until each step is actually done. Skip a step and the next technique doesn't unlock.

1

Watch

5-10 minute Ken video distilled from his live coaching. The principle, the framework, the example. Designed to be re-watched.

VIDEO · ~5-10 MIN
2

Read

3 to 5 named principles distilled from the video — the part you'll reference later when you've forgotten the words but need the rule. Each principle has a one-line answer.
