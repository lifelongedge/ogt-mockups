02 / Read The principles

  1. Scalping fits action-oriented operators with infrastructure.Highest cognitive load; tiny margins; not compatible with a day job.
  2. Day trading fits analytical traders with full daily attention.Significant time + emotional commitment; flat by close eliminates overnight risk.
  3. Swing trading fits patient, methodical mid-career professionals.Manageable alongside a day job; bears overnight gap risk.
  4. Position trading fits big-picture thinkers with capital.Lowest daily attention; longest drawdowns; largest required patience.
