Going-forward process

Step 1. For each new episode, I post the YouTube link with the start timestamp + the range I'd extract.

Step 2. You pick one of three treatments:

  • Raw — source video trimmed to the time range, no edits. Use when the visual is the point and stands alone.
  • A+S — Audio + Slides. Ken's audio extracted, typographic slides timed to narration. Use when the screen-share is unrelated noise.

  • PD — Public-domain override (Livermore, Seykota, pre-1972 footage).

Step 3. I build it your way and push.
