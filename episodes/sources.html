Going-forward process

Step 1. For each new episode, I post the YouTube link with the start timestamp + the range I'd extract.

Step 2. You pick one of three treatments:

  • Raw — source video trimmed to the time range, no edits. Use when the visual is the point and stands alone.
  • A+S — Audio + Slides. Ken's audio extracted, typographic slides timed to narration. Use when the screen-share is unrelated noise.
  • V+O — Video + Overlay. Source video plays; brief text overlays (top or bottom, ~60% width, centered) reinforce the key principle Ken is verbalizing. Use when the visual is relevant but the principle wants pinning down on screen.
  • PD — Public-domain override (Livermore, Seykota, pre-1972 footage).

Step 3. I build it your way and push.

V+O spec: overlays land medium-density (1 every 15–25s, one beat each). Locked style: optional gold Georgia headline + ink-white body on a dark scrim; position top|bottom per-overlay. Safe zones: bottom ≥12% from edge (clears controls), top ≥8% from edge (clears Zoom name tags). 350ms fade.
