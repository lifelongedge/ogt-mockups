EP. 01

Set Your Reason

Trading without a defined reason is volunteering to lose. Find the why before you risk a dollar.

01 / Watch Video

Dr. Ken Long — The Four Brothers (master craftsman identity) · clip 42:39–47:55 (~5:16)
Dr. Ken Long
Farmer → Blacksmith → Machinist → Trail Boss. Choosing your identity as a master craftsman is the deepest 'why'.

02 / Read The principles

  1. Name the why.Build wealth for a specific named purpose. Generic "make money" fails; "fund my mother's care" or "buy back my time at 55" sustains.
  2. Distinguish strong reasons from weak.Strong = intrinsic, multi-year, identity-anchored. Weak = comparison-based, dopamine-driven, externally validated.
  3. Write it down.A reason that lives only in your head is a reason that will rationalize itself away under pressure. Paper makes it real.
  4. Re-anchor weekly.Read your reason at the start of every Sunday planning session. The drift away from purpose is gradual; weekly re-anchoring catches it.

03 / Do Write your reason

Specific, named, multi-year, anchored to who you are. Minimum 80 characters; write what you'd want to read back to yourself during a 10% drawdown.
