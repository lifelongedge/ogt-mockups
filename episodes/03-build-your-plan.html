Build the Trading Plan

A trading plan written in your head is a plan that will rationalize itself away. Build the document; trade only what's in it.

01 / Watch Build tomorrow's trading plan

Dr. Ken Long
Live cohort planning — mindset first, then the daily report. Three categories: continuation, reversal, news.

02 / Read The principles

  1. Entry criteria — exact and binary.What specifically triggers a trade? Bullet-list it. "When I see a setup" is not a criterion; "break above prior-day high with volume > 1.5× 10-day avg" is.
  2. Position size rule — formula, not feel.Per the math-of-survival discipline: 1% account risk per trade, sized backward from the stop distance.
  3. Stop-loss rule — physical, immediate, structural.Where does the trade thesis prove false? That's the stop. Hard order, in the broker, at entry.
  4. Exit rule — predetermined targets and trail.Where do you scale? Where do you full-exit? Mechanical; not invented in the moment.
  5. Daily / weekly loss limit.Per the daily-loss-limit discipline: 2% account or 4R per day, whichever tighter. When hit, the day is over.
