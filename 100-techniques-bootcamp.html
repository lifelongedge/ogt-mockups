Questions you're already asking

FAQ

Why the monthly fee on top of the bundle?
The bundle pays for content; the monthly pays for the things that have ongoing cost — cloud-hosted simulators, weekly dashboard review by a coach, 48-hour email response, and Ken's monthly call. Charging once for those would mean either inflating the bundle past what most traders can absorb, or burning the coaching layer down to nothing once you're a year in. The monthly keeps the cohort sustainable.
What happens if I cancel the monthly?
You keep every technique you've paid for — videos, worksheets, completion records — forever. What you lose: live simulator access (the math runs on our servers), the coaching dashboard, instructor email, monthly call access, and the cohort community. You can resubscribe later if you want to climb actively again.
Is the monthly required, or just recommended?
For the bootcamp tier — required for the first 90 days minimum, so the coaching layer has time to make a meaningful difference. After that, cancel anytime. If you know up front you only want the curriculum, the course-only edition is the right product — no monthly at all.
How fast can I realistically finish?
Six months is aggressive but achievable for traders with 8-10 hours per week. Most cohort members finish the climb in 12-18 months, which we consider the healthy range — fast enough to build momentum, slow enough for each technique to become a habit. The bootcamp is designed for either pace.
Does Ken actually answer emails himself?
Strategic questions, yes — Ken personally responds to roughly 20% of instructor email, the ones flagged by the cohort coaches as needing founder-level judgment. The other 80% are answered by Ken-trained cohort coaches with the same 48-hour SLA. Every monthly group call is Ken live.
Can I switch from the course-only edition to the bootcamp later?
Yes. The course-only price ($3,495 or whatever you paid a la carte) credits 1:1 toward the bootcamp bundle. You activate the monthly when you upgrade. No double-charging for content you already own.
What's the refund policy?
Bundle: 60-day full refund, no questions. We want you to fully engage without feeling locked in. Monthly: cancel anytime, no prorated refund on the current month (since you've had access to the live coaching for that period). If you cancel within 48 hours of the monthly billing, we refund that cycle as a courtesy.
Do I need to connect a brokerage account?
Not required, and not until you're ready. The simulators run on synthetic + historical data by default. When you reach the Journeyman rung and want the Daily Limit Guard wired to your real platform, we support IBKR, TradeStation, and TC2000 via read-only API. Your account stays in your control — we never see balances or place trades.
