SCENE 5.2The VWAP Magnet MECHANIC

start 22:43 · ~90s clip

The specific tradable phenomenon that emerges from Scene 5.1. When a strong intraday move pulls price away from the VWAP AND leveraged holders start unwinding in the last 30 minutes, price gets pulled back to the VWAP — the "VWAP magnet." Ken names the measurement (FadeStat, FailStat) that quantifies this so you know how strong the pull will be before you take the trade.

Source: 2023-07-12 — Foundations Q&A Special  ·  Video ID: d_S1TCk2qnE
